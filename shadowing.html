Chomko & Rosier

Giving Memory To Bristol’s City Lights

Winner of Watershed’s 2014 Playable City Award, Shadowing enabling Bristol’s city lights to record and play back the shadows of those who passed underneath.

Shadowing offers an exploration of the disconnectedness that technology can create between strangers, the role of light in creating a city's character, and the unseen data layers and surveillance culture that pervades our contemporary urban spaces.

Shadowing is currently installed at London's Design Museum and has toured to York and Tokyo.
